# User Guide

Welcome to **Event Horizon**, the mission control center for your events.

## Getting Started

1.  **Access the Terminal:** Navigate to the homepage.
2.  **Sign Up / Login:**
    *   Click **Login** in the top navigation.
    *   You can create a new account or sign in with existing credentials.
    *   Once logged in, you will be redirected to your **Profile**.

## Profile Management

*   **Edit Profile:** Click on your avatar in the top right -> **Profile**.
*   **Avatar:** Upload a custom image to represent you in the system.
*   **Social Links:** Add links to your GitHub, LinkedIn, or other comms channels so other operatives can find you.

## Participating in Missions (Events)

### Finding Events
*   Go to **Missions** (Home).
*   Use the **Search** bar to filter by Mission Name.
*   Use the **Sector** (Location) filter to find events near you.

### Registering
1.  Click on an event card to view the **Mission Briefing**.
2.  Click **Initialize Registration**.
3.  If the organizer has set up custom protocols, answer the required questions.
4.  **Status:**
    *   **Registered:** You are confirmed.
    *   **Waitlisted:** The mission is at capacity. You are on standby. If a slot opens up, the organizer may approve you.

### My Missions
*   Go to **My Events**.
*   **Mission Assignments:** View events you are attending and your current status.
*   **Command Logs:** View events you are hosting.

## Organizing Missions

1.  Click **Create Event** in the navigation bar.
2.  **Mission Parameters:** Fill in Title, Description, Time, and Capacity.
3.  **Registration Protocols (Custom Questions):**
    *   You can add custom questions for attendees to answer during registration.
    *   Supported Types: Text, Checkbox, Long Text.
4.  **Managing Attendees:**
    *   Go to your event's detail page.
    *   View the **Roster**.
    *   **Approve** waitlisted users if space allows.
    *   **Cancel** registrations if necessary.
    *   **Export Data:** Download a CSV of all attendees and their answers.
